Privacy Policy for ezFlowcuz

📱 Local-first · No data collection

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ezFlowcuz (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Collection and Storage

ezFlowcuz does not collect, transmit, or upload any personal data to external servers. All data generated by your use of the App is stored locally on your device only, using the Hive database. The data includes:

  • Settings – App preferences such as first-time launch status, dark/light mode settings.
  • Tasks & Habits – Your to-do tasks, once tasks, habits, and related items.
  • Categories – Custom categories you create for organizing tasks and habits.
  • Timer Data – Selected timer presets and timer duration records.
  • Statistics – Historical data related to your habits, categories, monthly habit records, and once tasks.

All this information remains on your device. You have full control over this data, and you can delete it at any time by clearing the App’s data or uninstalling the App.

3. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect your data, the App uses third-party advertising services that may collect certain information to provide and improve ads. These services include:

📢 Google AdMob
📢 MobFox

These third-party services may automatically collect data such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (e.g., ad views, clicks)
  • Approximate location (if permission is granted)

This information is collected and processed by Google AdMob and MobFox in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by these third parties.

4. Data Security

Since all user-generated data remains locally on your device, we rely on the security features of your operating system and device to protect it. For third-party advertising services, they implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data they process.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page, and the “Effective Date” will be updated accordingly.
