1. Introduction

Welcome to ezFlowcuz (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about data practices.

2. Data Collection and Storage

ezFlowcuz does not collect, transmit, or upload any personal data to external servers. All data generated by your use of the App is stored locally on your device only, using the Hive database. You remain in full control, and you can delete all data at any time by clearing the App’s data or uninstalling the App.

The following types of information are stored locally on your device:

  • Settings — App preferences such as first-time launch status, dark/light mode selection, and language preference (e.g., if you select German, the App will remember this and automatically apply it the next time you open the App). Please note that translations may not be 100% accurate.
  • Tasks & Habits — Your to-do tasks, once tasks, habits, and all related items.
  • Categories — Custom categories you create for organizing tasks and habits.
  • Timer Data — Selected timer presets and timer duration records.
  • Statistics — Historical data related to your habits, categories, monthly habit records, and once tasks.
🔒 All this information remains strictly on your device. No cloud sync, no remote servers, no background transmission.

3. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect your personal data, the App uses third-party advertising services that may collect certain information to provide and improve ads. These services include:

• Google AdMob
• MobFox

These third-party services may automatically collect data such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (e.g., ad views, clicks, session interactions)
  • Approximate location (if permission is granted)

This information is collected and processed by Google AdMob and MobFox in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by these third parties. For more details about their data handling, please review their policies:

📄 Google AdMob Privacy Policy
📄 MobFox Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

Since all user-generated data remains locally on your device, we rely on the built-in security features of your operating system and device hardware to protect it. For third-party advertising services, they implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data they process. However, no system is 100% immune, and we encourage you to keep your device updated.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information (e.g., via advertising SDKs), please contact us so we can take appropriate action. We are committed to complying with applicable children’s privacy regulations.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page, and the “Effective Date” at the top will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
