Welcome to ezFlower ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

ezFlower does not collect, transmit, or upload any personal data to external servers. All data generated by your use of the App is stored locally on your device only, using the Hive database. The data includes:

  • Categories: Custom categories you create for organizing tasks and habits.

📀 All this information remains on your device. You have full control over this data, and you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.
